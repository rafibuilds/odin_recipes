Beef Stew

Description

Beef stew is a dish made by slowly cooking tough cuts of beef with vegetables like carrots, potatoes, onions, and celery in a rich broth, resulting in tender meat and a flavorful gravy, typically simmered until the ingredients are soft and the flavors meld together

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Garlic
  • White sugar
  • Thyme
  • Onions, carrots and celery
  • Tomato paste

Steps

  1. Cut the beef into cubes and cook the beef cubes in beef fat.
  2. Cook the onions, garlic and carrots in the pot with the beef.
  3. Put in some tomato paste.
  4. Trasfer all the contents to a pot. Boil everything down to a simmer.
  5. Serve it fresh and warm with mashed potatoes
