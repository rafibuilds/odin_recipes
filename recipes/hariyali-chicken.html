Hariyali Chicken

Description

Hariyali Chicken is an Indian dish featuring chicken marinated in a vibrant green paste. The marinade typically includes fresh mint, cilantro, coriander leaves, ginger, garlic, and green chilies. The chicken is then grilled or pan-cooked until tender and juicy.

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Fresh cilantro leaves
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Ginger, peeled and grated
  • Garlic, peeled and minced
  • Green chilies, seeded and chopped
  • Lemon juice
  • Garam masala
  • Ground cumin
  • Turmeric powder
  • Plain yogurt
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Combine mint leaves, cilantro leaves, coriander leaves, ginger, garlic, green chilies, lemon juice, garam masala, cumin, and turmeric powder in a blender or food processor. Blend into a smooth paste.
  2. Mix the chicken pieces with yogurt and the green paste. Marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight for deeper flavor.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the marinated chicken pieces and cook for 15-20 minutes, or until cooked through and browned.
  4. Serve hot with rice or naan.
